Description

This experiment investigates how different types of light affect plant growth, focusing on UV-A light and natural sunlight. Radish seeds will be planted in identical pots using the same soil and will be kept under controlled conditions such as equal water, temperature, and pot size. Each group of plants will be exposed to different light conditions, including natural sunlight and varying durations of UV-A light (4, 8, 12, and 24 hours). The plants will be watered equally every day, and their growth will be measured by recording their height in centimeters over a period of 1–2 weeks. By comparing the growth of plants under these different light conditions, the experiment aims to determine whether UV-A light affects plant growth more positively or negatively than sunlight.
